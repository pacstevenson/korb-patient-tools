How to Reach Us
⚠ Emergency
If you experience difficulty breathing, severe swelling, chest pain, a serious allergic reaction, or any other medical emergency — call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room immediately. Do not wait for a portal response.
Medical Questions — Patient Portal
For clinical questions about your treatment, side effects, or dosing, message your provider through the Tebra patient portal. This is a secure, HIPAA-compliant platform for medical questions only — please do not use the portal for scheduling, shipping, or billing inquiries. Portal messages are reviewed by your provider on their clinic days. Expect a response within 24 hours.
